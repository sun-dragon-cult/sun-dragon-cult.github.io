:::tip

All weapons included in the Chaosiums wiki are included as weapon items in the wiki module.

:::

Creating a weapon involves deciding if it can be used one handed, two handed, or a as a missile
weapon.

The weapons "usages" are linked to corresponding weapon skills. When you drag a weapon to an actor
sheet the linked weapon skills are automatically added to the character.
